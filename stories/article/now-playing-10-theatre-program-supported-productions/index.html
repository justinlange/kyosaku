Now Playing: 10 Theatre Program-Supported Productions

Posted by Nate von Zumwalt, Editorial Manager on Oct 29, 2013 at 01:10 pm.

The Sundance Institute Theatre Program’s breadth of support is tangible On- and Off-Broadway this fall and winter, with 10 productions developed in part by the program making theater runs. Additionally, the just-launched Sundance Institute Theatre Club allows subscribers and members to connect with Sundance-supported playwrights and their works, including four New York productions featured below (Fun Home, A Gentleman’s Guide to Love & Murder, Appropriate, and When We Were Young & Unafraid).  

(London)
The Drowned Man: A Hollywood Fable
formerly Woyzeck
presented by Punchdrunk & The National Theatre
Temple Studios
June 20‐December 31, 2013

(New York)
Fun Home
by Lisa Kron & Jeanine Tesori
directed by Sam Gold
Public Theater
October 1–November 3, 2013

(New York)
Marie Antoinette
by David Adjmi
directed by Rebecca Taichman
Soho Rep
October 2013
Starring Marin Ireland

(New York)
A Gentlemen’s Guide to Love & Murder
by Robert L. Freedman & Steven Lutvak
Broadway ‐ Walter Kerr Theater
Previews Begin: October 22
Opening Night: November 17
Starring Jefferson Mays

(New York)
When January Feels Like Summer
by Cori Thomas
Ensemble Studio Theatre
October 2013

(Washington DC)
Appropriate
by Branden Jacobs‐Jenkins
directed by Liesl Tommy
Woolly Mammoth Theatre Company
November 4–December 1, 2013

(New York)
Appropriate
by Branden Jacobs‐Jenkins
directed by Liesl Tommy
Signature Theatre Company
February-March 2014

(New York)
Little Miss Sunshine
by James Lapine & William Finn
directed by James Lapine
Second Stage Theatre
Begins October 2013

(New York)
When We Were Young & Unafraid
by Sarah Treem
Manhattan Theatre Club
Previews Begin: May 22, 2014
Opening Night, June 17, 2014
Starring Cherry Jones

(London)
The Body of an American
By Dan O’Brien
Gate Theatre
Part of the These American Lives Festival
January 16‐25, 2014

(Los Angeles)
Civilization (All You Can Eat) 
By Jason Grote
Sons of Semele
October 11‐November 10, 2013
