Theatre Program: The View from Manda

Photographer Philippa Ndisi-Hermann documented the 2010 Theatre Lab on Manda off the coast of Kenya last July. Sundance Institute East Africa is a program to support the work of theatre artists in East Africa, as well as to create exchange and exposure opportunities between U.S. artists and East African writers, directors, and performers.
