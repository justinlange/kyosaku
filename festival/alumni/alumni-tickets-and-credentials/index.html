Alumni Tickets & Credentials

ALUMNI CREDENTIALS

All Sundance Institute alumni are eligible for one complimentary 2014 Sundance Film Festival Alumni Credential (see requirements below).

Alumni Credentials provide access to:

  • All non-ticketed official Festival venues
  • Purchase up to 6 tickets at the Alumni Desk
  • Choose up to 2 event/party tickets at the Alumni Desk
  • 1 official 2014 Sundance Film Festival water bottle
  • Additional event tickets or sponsor give-aways at the Alumni Desk

ALUMNI CREDENTIALS – DEADLINE DECEMBER 13, 2013

Please email alumni@sundance.org by Friday, December 13, 2013, to pre-order a complimentary Alumni Credential. Pre-ordered credentials (by December 13) will be available for pick-up beginning January 15 at the Alumni Desk at Festival Headquarters. After December 13, Alumni Credentials are available for request onsite at the Alumni Desk during the Festival.

ALUMNI CREDENTIALS – AT THE FESTIVAL

After December 13, Alumni Credentials are available for request onsite at the Alumni Desk during the Festival. Come visit us at the Alumni Desk anytime during the Festival to request your credential. Depending on capacity, it takes about an hour to process.

Alumni Desk
Located near Film and Press Office Headquarters
Park City Marriot, Ballrooms 1895 Sidewinder Drive
Hours: 8:00 a.m. – 6:00 p.m.
January 16 – 26, 2014

ALUMNI TICKET PACKAGE – Tuesday, December 3, 2013

We are offering Alumni two Ticket Packages this year.  The first option is an Alumni Film Lovers Ticket Package which includes 12 tickets for $325 with no restriction on film category.  The second option is our Alumni Ticket Package A which includes 20 tickets and two Day One Awards Party tickets for $1000 with no restriction on film category between the dates of January 16-21, 2014.

The Alumni Sale begins Tuesday, December 3 and continues as inventory lasts until Thursday, December 5. Add FestivalTickets@sundance.org to your email contacts and look for the link to the purchase website the morning of Tuesday, December 3. Limited inventory; sales will be first-come, first-served.

Ticket Package ticket selection takes place between January 2-5.

Detailed instructions for how to select tickets will be emailed to package holders on December 18.

 

Alumni Film Lovers Ticket Package
Dates Valid: January 16-26
Cost: $325

  • Package includes 12 tickets to regular screenings and/or panel discussions.
  • Limit of 4 tickets total per screening or panel discussion (based upon availability).
  • Package holders will make their ticket selections online between January 2-5.
  • Package includes one non-pictured Festival Credential. (Credentials permit access to non-theatre venues including Filmmaker Lodge, Music Café, New Frontier, and Sundance House.
  • Several theatres are located outside of Park City (Salt Lake, Ogden, and Sundance Resort). Pay close attention to this when you make your ticket selections.
  • A non-refundable $30 processing fee will be applied to each Package.

 

Alumni Ticket Package A
Dates Valid: January 16-21
Cost: $1000

  • Package includes 20 tickets to regular screenings and/or panel discussions.
  • Package includes 1 Day One Party Ticket on January 16, 2014.
  • Limit of 4 tickets total per screening or panel discussion (based upon availability).
  • Package holders will make their ticket selections online between January 2-5.
  • Package includes one non-pictured Festival Credential. (Credentials permit access to non-theatre venues including Filmmaker Lodge, Music Café, New Frontier, and  Sundance House)
  • Several theatres are located outside of Park City (Salt Lake, Ogden, and Sundance Resort). Pay close attention to this when you make your ticket selections.
  • A non-refundable $30 processing fee will be applied to each Package.

 

Please note that, if you have a film screening in the 2014 Festival, you will receive additional information about ticketing opportunities from your Film Office Coordinator.

Any questions about Alumni Ticket Packages may be directed to the Sundance Ticketing Hotline at 888.285.7790 (toll-free) or 435.776.7878 or customerservice@sundance.org. Questions about Sundance Alumni Programs can be directed to alumni@sundance.org.

ALUMNI TICKETS AT THE FESTIVAL

With an Alumni Credential, each alumni may purchase up to six tickets (per credential) onsite at the Alumni Desk during the Festival. This is a special alumni-only pool of tickets, and sales will be first-come, first-served.

PARTY INVITES

We have a select number of event invitations set aside for our alumni during the Festival to meet other filmmakers, artists, composers, and industry. With an Alumni Credential, each alumni may request two tickets (per credential) onsite at the Alumni Desk. We’ll have a limited number of tickets to select official Sundance events including Opening and Closing Night Party, Native Party, and Shorts Party.

All requests must be done in person at the Alumni Desk, and are on a first-come, first-served basis.
