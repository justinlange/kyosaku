Accreditation

We invite members of the press to cover the Sundance Film Festival from offsite or to request onsite press accreditation.  The accreditation application for the Sundance Film Festival can be found here and will be open until December 10th, 2013.  To complete the application form, we ask members of the press for basic background information, as well as relevant film work samples and a coverage plan for the Sundance Film Festival.

Interview Requests

The Press Office is here to coordinate interviews with Festival programmers, Institute staff, operations personnel and other officials, as well as with filmmakers, New Frontier artists, musicians, talent and crew. Email press@sundance.org with your specific request, deadline and press outlet for which you are on assignment. We will do our best to accommodate all requests, recognizing it is not always possible to do so. In order to meet press deadlines, advanced notice is always appreciated.

We also have limited space available to us for these interviews; please contact us at least 24 hours in advance if you are in need of interview space.
