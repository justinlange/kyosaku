Sponsor Venues and Activities

 
Festival Co-op
608 Main St

Join us at the Festival Co-op on Main Street. Visit with some of our 2014 Festival sponsors, including Canada Goose, L’Oréal Paris and other supporters and participate in a variety of other offerings and demonstrations.

Open daily from 10:00 a.m. to 6:00 p.m.

Sundance House Presented by HP
638 Park Ave.

Come and visit the Sundance House Presented by HP for an experience that will put you at the heart of the filmmaking journey. From the earliest stages of production through to your viewing of a finished movie, HP is present every step of the way. Throughout the venue, you will be able to participate in a variety of interactive experiences that will allow you to explore the ways in which technology is playing an increasingly pivotal role in the film industry. You can also get out of the cold and experience our Internet Café with free Wi-Fi, laptops, and printers available for your use.

Take the weight off your snow boots and drop into the HP Live Lounge- an extension of the HP experience. Our heated tent is located in the heart of Park City and provides great views up Main Street. Enjoy a cup of hot cocoa, share your Festival photos, relax, and enjoy music from various local artists taking the stage.

The Sundance House and HP Live Lounge are open to the public;
No accreditation is required*

Sundance House Open daily from 10:00 a.m.
HP Live Lounge Open daily from 9:30 a.m.

Acura Lounge
Corner of Main & Heber

Acura is proud to continue its support of the Sundance Institute and serve as the Official Vehicle of the 2014 Sundance Film Festival. Visit Acura’s space to see innovation come to life, as we keep you in the know on the latest in Festival news and trends. Plus, recharge with gourmet movie snacks and enjoy music at the Festival in an unexpected new way.

Sundance Channel HQ
268 Main St

Eavesdrop in the cafe, connect the dots at afternoon panel conversations with writers, actors, directors and industry experts, or just meet up before you head out – the lounge is particularly friendly from 4 to 6 pm. Open 10 to 6 pm daily. Check out @sundancechannel – for the who, what, when, where, why.

Chase Sapphire on Main
528 Main St

Films are just the beginning with Chase Sapphire Preferred®. Go on location and sample the best plates from local restaurants as you explore “A Taste of Park City” and browse our gallery of exclusive memorabilia. We’ll give you a whole new perspective on your favorite Festival films of the past in this one-of-a-kind experience you won’t want to miss. Feel free to speak to our customer service specialists about how to make the most of your time on set, and go home with stories to tell. Open daily from 11:00a.m. to 5:00 p.m.

YouTube on Main Street
596 Main St

An escape from the cold for creatives and innovators alike. Stop by YouTube on Main Street to join the next generation of the filmmaking community. Daily happenings include a live Sundance Film Festival production, screenings, panels, happy hours, and much more. We are here for filmmakers, creators, and artists to mingle, share and get inspired. Open: Thursday, January 16 - Sunday, January 26, 2014, 10:00 a.m. - 10:00 p.m., Sunday, January 26, 2014, 10:00 a.m. - 1:00 p.m.

Click here to see a complete schedule of events and programming at YouTube on Main Street.

Open to all Festival Credential Holders and invited guests as space allows.

How To Be Successful on YouTube
New Frontier Microcinema
Tuesday Jan 21
3-4:30pm

In this presentation, developed with YouTube's top creators, learn the key strategies filmmakers can use for creating successful content on YouTube that keeps audiences engaged and coming back for more.  The session will explore questions you should ask yourself when developing new online content, and provide examples of how YouTube offers filmmakers the opportunity to have creative freedom and reach a wide audience.

Airbnb Haus
628 Park Ave

Travel inspires the best stories we create and share. From treehouses to castles, Airbnb gives access to the most interesting places to stay around the world, placing you in the local scene and connecting you with unforgettable characters to be part of your story.  We invite you into our home in the heart of the Sundance Film Festival for moments of creative inspiration, access to special events, and some good old-fashioned hospitality.

Check out the full schedule of events at Airbnb Haus

Open 10 a.m.–6 p.m.

Adobe Presents Engaging Story, Brilliant Visuals, Low Budget- The Changing Face of Independent Film
New Frontier Microcinema

Friday, January 17, 3:00 p.m.- 4:30 p.m.

Low budget doesn’t have to mean low-quality for independent film. In this panel, meet up-and-coming filmmakers who have created entertaining and high-quality independent films on undeniably low- even micro- budgets. Hear about the latest technology trends for budget-conscious indie filmmakers, including the cameras and integrated software solutions, that make stunning storytelling possible. Learn how brilliant visuals, awe-inspiring stories and high quality filmmaking are possible, even on a low budget.

Keeping you Hydrated

Brita® FilterForGood® is back for the sixth year to help reduce waste and keep you hydrated in the high altitudes of Park City! Be sure to pick up your free reusable Nalgene® bottle and fill it up with Brita® filtered water at hydration stations throughout the Festival. Remember, you need to drink more water at high altitudes, so aim to refill 6 to 8 times per day to acclimate to the elevation. To learn more on the health benefits of water, “like” Brita® FilterForGood® on Facebook.

Canon Spotlight: A Conversation on the Craft of Cinematography
New Frontier Microcinema

Monday, January 20th, 3:00-4:30 p.m.

Join Canon U.S.A., Inc. for a panel discussion and screening with some of today’s most celebrated filmmakers, spotlighting the craft of cinematography and the tools that help bring their stories to life. Panelists will share valuable insights, anecdotes, and technical knowledge, as well as discuss their real-world experience with the Canon Cinema EOS line of professional digital cameras and lenses. Visit the festival website for participant updates. Whether you make documentaries, narrative features, television shows, commercials, web series, or any type of creative motion pictures, Canon delivers a set of tools for discerning filmmakers to capture compelling visuals and emotional reality.

The Hub Presented by the Utah Film Commission
751 Main St

Attendees and participants of the festival are invited to come and experience Utah. THE HUB is a hospitality spot where we have warm drinks and complimentary snacks on hand. Join us in discovering why the Beehive State is a superb destination for business, tourism, and film. Open daily from 10:00 a.m. to 4:00 p.m.

The MorningStar Farms® Veggie Burger Bar
317 Main St

Stop in for a FREE hot meal served up veggie style at the MorningStar Farms® Veggie Burger Bar. Try one of our great tasting veggie burgers with gourmet toppings, hickory BBQ riblet sliders, veggie chili or chik’n buffalo wings. Vegan options available, too. Find us across the street from the Egyptian Theater at 317 Historic Main Street.

The MorningStar Farms® Veggie Burger Bar is open to the public Thursday, January 16 through Tuesday, January 21 from 11:00 am to 11:00 p.m.

The Source:  A Conversation on the Origin and Future of Creativity in the 21st Century:  Doug Aitken and Maurice Marciano
Saturday, January 18, 12-1:30 p.m.

To celebrate the U.S. premiere of Doug Aitken's project, The Source (evolving), the artist, with art collector, Maurice Marciano, will discuss how ideas begin and the future of creativity in the 21st century.  Taking The Source as a point of departure, this dialogue will be a lively exploration across the creative disciplines-- film, visual arts, music and architecture.
