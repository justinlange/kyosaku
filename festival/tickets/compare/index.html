Festival
Passes
Festival Passes give you priority access to screenings without having to select tickets in advance of the Festival. Simply show up at the screening of your choice at least 30 minutes prior to the published screening start time to gain priority entrance to the theatre. All Pass Holders must be seated 15 minutes prior to the published screening start time to guarantee their seats. LEARN MORE >>
Ticket
Packages
Ticket Package Holders have the first chance to select tickets prior to the Festival. Designed for two or more people, each Ticket Package comes with a set number of tickets and two Festival Credentials. LEARN MORE >>
Individual
Tickets
Looking to add a couple extra tickets to your Ticket Package? Or maybe you're looking to attend a few screenings during the second half of the Festival? Individual Tickets are the remaining tickets from Ticket Package selection. Advance Ticket availability for the first half of the Festival is extremely limited, but there are still films to see during the second half of the Festival. Buy online up to 36 hours before showtime or at the Main Box Office up to 2 hours before showtime. Newly released  tickets are available daily during the festival at 8:00am at the Park City and Salt Lake City Main Box Offices. The best part? Individual Tickets are only $20 each. LEARN MORE >>
Waitlist
Tickets
The Waitlist is a Sundance tradition. Although many films appear to be “sold-out” or if you can’t make it to a Main Box Office, you can still join our electronic eWaitlist at two hours before a scheduled showtime and just show up directly at the theatre. Register for the Sundance Film Festival eWaitlist at ewaitlist.sundance.org. More information is here. Waitlist tickets are only $15 each (cash only). LEARN MORE >>
Credentials
Get access to our Festival Venues! Credentials permit access to non-theatre venues including Filmmaker Lodge, Sundance ASCAP Music Café, Festival Co-Op, New Frontier, and Sundance House. LEARN MORE >>
Utah
Locals
Year-round Utah Locals get special access to Festival Passes, Ticket Packages, and Individual Tickets. Click here to learn more about our specially designed Locals options. LEARN MORE >>
