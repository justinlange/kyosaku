Attend Next Fest

Celebrate the renegade spirit of independent filmmaking with four days of films, music, and summertime fun.

Home Base
Kick-Off

ACE Hotel Downtown Los Angeles

The home base for Sundance NEXT FEST is the recently opened Theatre at Ace Hotel Downtown Los Angeles. Originally opened in the 1920s as the United Artists Theatre, the space symbolizes a coming together of independent artists and perfectly embodies the spirit of Sundance NEXT FEST.

From Friday August 8 – Sunday August 10 Sundance NEXT FEST presents LA premieres of films paired with live music and conversations with cultural icons.

Sundance Institute Members will have first access to purchase tickets. Join Today.

Hollywood Forever Cemetery

Sundance NEXT FEST kicks off with an outdoor screening of a Sundance cult classic at one of LA’s coolest cinematic venues. The screening takes place at Hollywood Forever Cemetery in conjunction with Cinespia.

Sundance Institute Members will have first access. Join Today.

Press

Sundance NEXT FEST, Presented by Sundance Institute takes place Aug. 7-10, 2014 at Ace Hotel Los Angeles and Hollywood Forever Cemetery.

Featuring screenings, music and conversation, Sundance NEXT FEST is an extension of the popular NEXT <=> section at the Sundance Film Festival in Park City, Utah, which showcases stylistically adventurous films that take a bold approach to storytelling.
